## 1. TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF USE

### 1.1 Purpose and acceptance
WSPACE is a technology platform that acts as an intermediary between people offering physical spaces by the hour (WSpacer+) and people wishing to book them (WSpacer). By registering and using the platform, you fully accept these Terms and Conditions. If you do not agree, you must not use the service.

### 1.2 Definitions
- **WSPACE:** the technology platform this document refers to.
- **WSpacer:** a registered user who searches for and books spaces.
- **WSpacer+:** a registered user who additionally publishes and manages their own or authorized spaces.
- **Space:** a property or physical area published for hourly rental (office, meeting room, coworking, creative space, music rehearsal room, among others).
- **Booking:** an accepted request to use a Space for a specific date and time range.

### 1.3 Nature of the service
WSPACE is **not the owner, lessor, or manager** of the published Spaces. Its role is strictly that of a technology intermediary that facilitates the match between supply (WSpacer+) and demand (WSpacer), payment processing through authorized third parties, and managing communication and ratings between the parties.

### 1.4 Registration and user accounts
1. Using the platform requires registering with truthful, complete, and up-to-date information.
2. The user is responsible for the confidentiality of their password and for all activity carried out from their account.
3. Registration by minors is prohibited. WSPACE may request age verification and suspend accounts that violate this condition.
4. To acquire the WSpacer+ role, the user must provide an identity document and a bank account certificate, as an identity-verification and payment-enablement mechanism.

### 1.5 Roles and responsibilities

**Of the WSpacer+ (host):**
- Publish truthful information about the Space (real photos, capacity, location, availability, and amenities).
- Keep availability up to date and honor approved bookings.
- Respond to booking requests within the deadline set by the platform.
- Comply with the tax obligations arising from providing the Space rental service, according to their tax regime (see section 1.7).

**Of the WSpacer (booking user):**
- Use the booked Space in line with its intended purpose and the conditions disclosed by the host.
- Make the corresponding payment through the means enabled by the platform.
- Respect the Space's hours and rules of use.

### 1.6 Booking process
1. The WSpacer selects a Space, an available date and time, and submits a booking request.
2. The WSpacer+ has a deadline defined within the platform to approve or reject the request.
3. If the request isn't answered within that deadline, it is cancelled automatically at no charge.
4. Once the booking is approved, the corresponding charge is processed through the enabled payment gateway.

### 1.7 Prices, fees, and VAT
1. The Space's price is set freely by the WSpacer+.
2. WSPACE charges a technology-intermediation service fee to both the WSpacer and the WSpacer+ on every booking actually made, together with the VAT applicable to that fee, in accordance with current tax regulations.
3. **WSPACE does not act as a withholding agent for VAT arising from the Space rental service itself.** The price published by the WSpacer+ must include, where applicable, the VAT proper to their own activity, with sole responsibility for its settlement and filing with DIAN (Colombia's national tax authority) resting with them, according to their tax regime.
4. WSPACE only settles and, where applicable, files the VAT corresponding to its own intermediation fee.

### 1.8 Cancellation policy and penalties
1. The WSpacer may cancel a confirmed booking within whatever window the platform sets as a no-cost cancellation.
2. Cancellations made outside that window, as well as non-compliance by the WSpacer+ (unjustified rejection, the space not actually being available, among others), may result in financial penalties, which will be disclosed to the user before being confirmed.

### 1.9 Obligations and misuse
The following are prohibited: (i) publishing false or misleading information about a Space; (ii) using the platform for purposes other than renting physical spaces by the hour; (iii) contacting the other party to arrange payment outside the platform in order to avoid fees; (iv) any conduct that violates third-party rights or applicable law.

### 1.10 Intellectual property
The WSPACE name, its logo, interface design, and other distinctive elements belong to its developers and may not be reproduced without authorization.

### 1.11 Limitation of liability
WSPACE does not guarantee the absolute accuracy of information published by WSpacer+ users, nor is it liable for damages, losses, or harm arising from use of a Space, which are the direct responsibility of the parties involved in each booking. WSPACE acts as a technology facilitator, not as a party to the underlying rental agreement.

### 1.12 Personal data protection
The processing of users' personal data is governed by the Personal Data Processing Policy, available in section 2 of this document, which forms an integral part of these Terms and Conditions.

### 1.13 Minors
Use of WSPACE is restricted to people over 18 years of age, with legal capacity to enter into contracts.

### 1.14 Amendments
WSPACE may amend these Terms at any time, notifying users through the platform. Continued use of the service after notification implies acceptance of the changes.

### 1.15 Governing law and jurisdiction
These Terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Colombia. Any dispute will be submitted to the competent courts of the Republic of Colombia, without prejudice to the consumer-protection mechanisms established under Law 1480 of 2011.

### 1.16 Support channels (PQRSD — requests, complaints, claims, suggestions, and reports)
Users may submit requests, complaints, claims, suggestions, or reports through the contact channel enabled on the platform, which will be handled within the applicable legal timeframes.

## 2. PERSONAL DATA PROCESSING POLICY (HABEAS DATA)

*Drafted in accordance with Article 15 of the Colombian Constitution, Law 1581 of 2012, and Decree 1074 of 2015.*

### 2.1 Data controller
WSPACE, as data controller, collects and processes its users' personal data in accordance with this policy.

### 2.2 Data collected
- **Identification data:** first name, last name, national ID (WSpacer+ only), phone number, email.
- **Verification data (WSpacer+):** identity document, bank certificate.
- **Usage data:** booking history, ratings, messages tied to a booking.
- **Technical data:** IP address, device type, language preference (for interface personalization purposes, e.g. the ES/EN selector).

### 2.3 Purposes of processing
1. Manage user registration and authentication.
2. Process bookings, payments, and fee settlement.
3. Verify WSpacer+ identity to enable space publishing.
4. Send service-related notifications (confirmations, reminders, responses to requests).
5. Handle requests, complaints, and claims.
6. Statistical purposes and service improvement, in aggregated and/or anonymized form.

### 2.4 Data subject's authorization
At the time of registration, the user must expressly accept the processing of their personal data under this policy, through an unambiguous mechanism (a checkbox that is not pre-checked).

### 2.5 Rights of the data subject
Under Law 1581 of 2012, the data subject has the right to:
1. Know, update, and correct their personal data.
2. Request proof of the authorization granted.
3. Be informed about how their data has been used.
4. File complaints with the Superintendency of Industry and Commerce (SIC) for violations of the law.
5. Revoke the authorization and/or request deletion of the data, when no legal or contractual duty prevents it.
6. Access their personal data free of charge.

### 2.6 Procedure for exercising these rights
Requests may be submitted through the platform's contact channel. WSPACE will respond to inquiries within the following ten (10) business days, and to claims within the following fifteen (15) business days of receipt, in accordance with legal timeframes.

### 2.7 Data transfer and transmission
Data may be shared with:
- The enabled payment gateway (ePayco), solely for transaction processing.
- Competent authorities, when legally required.
Personal data will not be sold or shared with third parties for purposes other than those set out here, without the data subject's prior authorization.

### 2.8 Information security
WSPACE implements reasonable technical measures to protect personal data against loss, misuse, or unauthorized access, including password hashing and restricted access to sensitive information.

### 2.9 Retention period
Data will be kept for as long as the user maintains an active account and, afterward, for the term legally required to address any legal or contractual requirements.

### 2.10 Sensitive data and minors
WSPACE does not collect sensitive data (health, racial origin, sexual orientation, religious or political beliefs, among others) nor data belonging to minors, since the service is restricted to people over 18.
